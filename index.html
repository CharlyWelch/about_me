Hello! Thanks for playing my little game.

My name is Charly. I like to spend my time adventuring outdoors, hiking, camping, and exploring the Pacific Northwest. Science is my jam, and I love making things for the people I care about. Some of my favorite hobbies are wooburning and carving, wool felting, and painting with various media (including blood). I'm mama bear to the most amazing seven year old, Oliver. I LOVE cooking and eating with my family and community.

I've done a good amount of traveling here in the US, mostly by car or bus or train, but have never left the continent. I did visit Toronto once, but that was in a different age, when you could drive across the border with just your state ID! Some day, I'll travel the world with my kiddo. I want to see how life has evolved differently on the other sides of the planet.
